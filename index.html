Meat Vegetables Fruits

–     Salata orientala    –



  • 500g potato
  • 3 egs
  • 1 red onion
  • 200g pikles cucumbers
  • two tablespoon oil
  • parsley
  • a teaspoonful of vinegar
  • peper, salt

Mod de preparare

Put water in a pot and add salt to taste, in the water you must put the the potatoes for boling for about 20 minutes and the egs for about 5 minutes. While boiling potatos cut the onion julien and mix with salt. Parsley is chopped finely according to preference. Pikles cucumbers cut round and mix with onion. After the potatoes and eggs are boiled, chop and mix with the onions and cucumbers. The oile is mixed with vinegar and peper. At the end this dressing is mixed with the other ingredients you have already prepared. Add more salt if is needed.
